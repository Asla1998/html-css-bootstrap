ERNAKULAM


Sprawling, residential Ernakulam is known for Marine Drive, a busy waterfront promenade where boats offer backwater cruises. The Kerala Folklore and Hill Palace museums explore local heritage through art and antiquities. Shops along Broadway and on MG Road sell fabrics, crafts, and spices, while modern Lulu Mall also has a cinema and an ice rink.


Destination

Top Destinations

Hill Palace Museum

Hill Palace is an archaeological museum and palace located in the Tripunithura neighbourhood of Kochi city in the Indian state of Kerala.

LuLu International Shopping Malls Private Limited Kochi

LuLu Mall Kochi is a shopping mall located in Edappally, Kochi, Kerala. Spanning 17 acres, it is one of the largest malls in India with a total built up area of 171,870.624 m² and a retail space of 68,000 m².

Mangalavanam Bird Sanctuary

Mangalavanam is an ecologically sensitive area situated at the centre of the Indian city of Kochi, covering about 2.74 hectares.

Subhash Bose Park

Popularly Subhash Park, the Subhash Chandra Bose Park is located in the heart of Ernakulam city. Overlooking the Vembanand Lake and Cochin Harbour, it is major hangouts for the city dwellers.

Fort Kochi Beach

Fort Kochi beach is a beach along the Arabian Sea situated in Fort Kochi in the city of Kochi in South India, Kerala state.

Munambam Beach

Munambam beach is among the quaint spots of Vypeen island, situated near the Munambam fishing harbour in Ernakulam district.

Kumbalangi

Kumbalangi is an island village in the outskirts of Kochi city in the state of Kerala, India. Situated amidst backwaters, Kumbalangi is a major tourist attraction and is famous for its Chinese fishing nets.

Mattancherry

Mattancherry, is a locality in the city of Kochi, India. It is about 9 km south-west from the city center.The place where erstwhile 'Ancherry Mattom' located is now a Tamil Brahmin settlement.

Wonderla Amusement Park, Kochi

Left or right, wherever you look, you can hear giggles, screams of joy and shouts of excitement! Wonderla Kochi is 30 acres of pure, fun-filled joyrides.
